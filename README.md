# TradingView Market Structure Dashboard

A TradingView Pine Script project that combines higher-timeframe market structure, EMA analysis, trading sessions, and economic news into a single dashboard.

The goal of this project is **not** to generate buy or sell signals. Instead, it helps traders reduce repetitive analysis and quickly understand the overall market context before making a trading decision.

---

## Why I Built This

While reviewing my trades, I noticed that many losing trades were not caused by poor setups—they were caused by missing the bigger picture.

Before entering a trade, I repeatedly had to check:

* Higher timeframe market structure
* EMA alignment
* Active trading session
* Upcoming high-impact economic news

Switching between multiple charts and websites made the process repetitive and increased the chances of missing important information.

To simplify this workflow, I built this dashboard.

---

* 📈 1H Market Structure Trend
